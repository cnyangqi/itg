NpsSitemap class

NpsSitemap used to generate the Google Sitemap.Sitemaps protocol enables you to send URL list to search engines.

The index file name seems like sitemapindex1.xml,sitemapindex2.xml,etc. At the first time, you need to login to google and verify your sitemaps. After that,all the sitemap will send to google for update automatically.
